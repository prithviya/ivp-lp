About EDII

The Entrepreneurship Development and Innovation Institute (EDII), established in 2001 and based in Chennai, plays a crucial role in fostering entrepreneurship and promoting self-employment across Tamil Nadu. Operating under the Department of Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSME), EDII is dedicated to providing entrepreneurship education and enabling innovation for startups and businesses.

What is the Innovation Voucher Programme (IVP)?

The Innovation Voucher Programme (IVP) is designed to promote research, innovation, and market entry for startups and MSMEs in Tamil Nadu. Through this scheme, businesses can access government grants to fund their innovative projects.

Voucher Categories

Voucher Purpose Grant Amount Govt. Contribution
Voucher A Entry into Research Up to ₹2 Lakhs 80% of the Project Cost
Voucher B Entry into Market Up to ₹5 Lakhs 50% of the Project Cost

Eligibility Criteria

The IVP is open to startups and MSMEs that meet the following requirements:

  • Must be a manufacturing or service-based MSME/Startup.
  • Must be registered and located in Tamil Nadu.
  • Must be willing to co-finance the innovation investment.
  • Must fall within the sectors supported by AIC RAISE.
